WHY INVEST IN THE SLAPCHIP CO?

Over 22 years of franchising experience enables us to offer complete franchisee support, providing a turnkey solution - from site selection to staff recruitment and training.

WE ARE WITH YOU EVERY STEP OF THE WAY

The initial training program is 6 weeks, facilitated by dedicated operations trainers. An extensive Operations Manual, together with diagrammatic in-store recipe, process and systems cards provide both yourself and your management with quick reference and self-help tools on a wide variety of items. Training and weekly support from experienced Regional Managers, assisted by Regional Kitchen Technicians, provide assistance from detailed kitchen processes, through to benchmarking and business planning.

GLOBAL FRANCHISING EXPERIENCE SINCE 1988

With decades of franchising expertise we are able to offer comprehensive support for each of our Franchisees. We have a number of different store types available for the South African market, depending on your location, investment and needs.

FRANCHISEE PROFILE

A successful business is driven by a hands-on individual who understands the importance of superior service delivery, is passionate about people and who is willing to commit to a long-term plan that will yield results.
